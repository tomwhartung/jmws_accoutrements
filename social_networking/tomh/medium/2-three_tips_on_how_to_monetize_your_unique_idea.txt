
 Three Tips on How to Monetize Your Unique Idea
================================================

1. Read The Tipping Point by Malcolm Gladwell
-  People will go for just about anything unique

2. Decide what type of website to build
-  Table of website types and how to monetize each (from google).

3. Read To Sell Is Human by Daniel Pink
-  Learn how the internet has changed how products are sold
-  Learn how to write the pitches, etc.
