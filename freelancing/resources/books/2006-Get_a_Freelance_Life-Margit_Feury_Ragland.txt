Structure of an Article (pp. 189-191)
-------------------------------------
Hed: Headline
Dek: A few sentences under the headline
Lede: First sentence, or first few sentences, meant to pull readers in
Nut Graf: A paragraph or two that summarizes the article
Narrative Structure: Varies according to type of article
Body: The bulk of the story
Kicker: Last sentence or paragraph, where everything ties together
Sidebar or box: Contains additional information
Quotes: Add life but do not over-use
