Saving Your Results

The SeeOurMinds.com quiz allows you to store your quiz results on our server under your email address. Using this option, the quiz page enables you to save your answers on our server and retrieve, change, and add to them when you return.

Note that using your email address to save your quiz results does not sign you up for our newsletter. To sign up for our newsletter, use one of the forms on the site that is labeled specifically for that purpose.

Why Save Your Results?

The ability to save your results on our server exists solely for your convenience.

Saving them allows you to, for example, save your answers for a shorter quiz and come back later and answer additional questions. This feature also lets you go back and change a few answers to see how that might effect the score. Finally, saving them also means you can access your results at any time without needing to write them down.
