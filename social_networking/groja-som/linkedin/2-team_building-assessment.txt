Part 3: Team Building Step 1: Assessment
========================================
This step contains the following sub-steps.

(1) To determine whether my theory is accurate, at least one person needs to fill out
    an evaluation form before the process, so that we can determine whether there is an improvement
(2) Each team member answers a questionnaire that yields a four-letter type score
(3) I use the program to draw an image based on the score

Each of these sub-steps should take about 15 minutes.
